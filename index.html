我们的使命：从咨询洞察到价值共创

Our Mission: From Consulting Insight to Co-Creating Value

NES集团自1994年成立以来，在智慧城市与建筑领域积累了深厚的咨询顾问经验。但我们意识到，真正的价值并不仅仅是“提供建议”，而是在于“创造并拥有价值”。

Since our founding in 1994, NES Group has built deep consulting expertise in smart cities and buildings. However, we realized that true value isn't just about "giving advice," but about "creating and owning value."

因此，我们的使命已经升华：我们不再仅仅交付咨询报告，而是与客户共同构建、投资并交付定义未来的核心技术产品。 这一转变标志着我们从服务提供商转变为客户的长期价值共创伙伴，从一次性项目交付迈向可沉淀、可复利的价值创造。

Therefore, our mission has evolved: We no longer just deliver consulting reports. We partner with our clients to build, invest in, and deliver the core technology products that define the future. This marks our transformation from a service provider to a long-term value co-creator, moving from one-off projects to creating compounding, lasting value.

我们通过“核心技术自研”和“全球产业投资”双轮驱动，确保我们提供的不仅是当下的解决方案，更是通向未来的技术蓝图。

Through a dual-wheel drive of "Core Technology R&D" and "Global Industry Investment," we ensure our offerings are not just solutions for today, but a clear roadmap to the future.

智慧城市与OS事业群

Smart City & OS Group

该事业群构建城市的“数字大脑”。通过融合BIM（建筑信息模型）、IoT实时数据和AI分析，我们为城市管理者、机场和大型园区构建一个可交互、可仿真的数字孪生平台。这使得“运营仿真”、“预测性维护”和“应急响应”成为可能，全面提升运营效率和安全性。

This group builds the "digital brain" for cities. By fusing BIM (Building Information Modeling), real-time IoT data, and AI analytics, we create an interactive and simulatable Digital Twin platform for city managers, airports, and large campuses. This enables "Operational Simulation," "Predictive Maintenance," and "Emergency Response," significantly enhancing operational efficiency and safety.

智慧交通事业群

Smart Transportation Group

我们不仅仅是提供交通咨询。我们开发基于AI的“交通代理”（Traffic AI Agents），可部署在边缘计算网关上。这些智能体能够实时分析视频流，动态优化交叉路口信号灯、识别交通事故，并通过V2X（车路协同）技术，为未来的自动驾驶提供关键决策支持，有效缓解城市拥堵。

We do more than just transport consulting. We develop AI-based "Traffic AI Agents" deployable on edge computing gateways. These agents analyze video streams in real-time to dynamically optimize traffic signals, detect accidents, and provide critical decision support for autonomous driving via V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) technology, effectively alleviating urban congestion.

Smart Aviation Group

机场是技术集成度最高的“小型城市”。我们提供“智慧机场数字孪生SaaS平台”，帮助机场运营商实现：

Airports are "mini-cities" with the highest degree of technological integration. We provide a "Smart Airport Digital Twin SaaS Platform" that helps airport operators achieve:

  • 运营仿真与优化： 模拟旅客流动和行李处理，优化资源配置。
  • Operational Simulation & Optimization: Simulate passenger flow and baggage handling to optimize resource allocation.

  • Sustainability (ESG) Management: Track and manage carbon emissions in real-time to help achieve "Green Airport" goals.
